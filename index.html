About Me

"Hello! I’m a game development student from Malmö University, poised to graduate with hands-on experience in creating engaging games. I've crafted platformers, a unique online multiplayer arena game, and am particularly proud of "Detached", a coop puzzle game that my team and I are prepping for a Steam release. My skills encompass C# and C++, with a strong proficiency in Unity. Beyond the screen, I indulge in competitive games such as CSGO and Valorant, and discipline myself further with kickboxing, an activity that reinforces resilience and focus. As I look to the future, my goal is clear: to continually refine my programming expertise and create games that resonate, both with wider audiences and my personal gaming preferences."
